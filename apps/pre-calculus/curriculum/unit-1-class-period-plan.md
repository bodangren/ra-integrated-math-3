# Unit 1 Class-Period Plan

This is the source-backed class-period plan for AP Precalculus Unit 1: Polynomial and Rational Functions.

## Planning Source Hierarchy

1. CED topic and competency identity from `source/college-board/ced-topic-index.json`.
2. CED clarification/guidance errata from `source/college-board/clarification-guidance.md`.
3. Passwater local instructional evidence from `source/passwater/unit-1.md`.

## Pacing Rationale

The Passwater source recommends 24–34 days for the 14 CED topics. The course budget allocates **41 instruction days** per unit (out of ~165 total). Each topic is paced by content depth:

| Topic | Passwater Rec | Instruction Days | Rationale |
|-------|:---:|:---:|-----------|

| 1.2 Rates of Change | 2 | 3 | 4 notes pages, 5 worksheets (A–E), AROC formula + secant line |
| 1.3 ROC in Linear/Quadratic | 2 | 3 | 6 notes pages, 3 worksheets, second differences + concavity |
| 1.4 Polynomial Functions | 2 | 3 | 5 notes pages, 2 worksheets, extrema, inflection points, rate-of-change language |
| 1.5 Complex Zeros | 2–3 | 3 | 6 notes pages, 5 worksheets, FTA + conjugates + sign charts |

| 1.8 Rational Zeros | 1–2 | 3 | 4 notes pages, 2 worksheets, 7-step inequality method |
| 1.9 Vertical Asymptotes | 1–2 | 3 | 3 notes pages, 3 worksheets, one-sided limits |
| 1.10 Holes | 1–2 | 3 | 3 notes pages, worksheets, holes vs VAs distinction |
| 1.11 Equivalent Representations | 2–3 | 3 | 6 notes pages, 3 worksheets, long division + Binomial Thm |

| **Subtotal** | **24–34** | **38** | |

Assessment breaks follow the Passwater unit guide:

- **Quiz 1.1–1.3** after topic 1.3 (1 day)
- **Review + Test 1.1–1.6** after topic 1.6 (2 days)
- **Review + Test 1.7–1.11** after topic 1.11 (2 days)
- **Final review + Test 1.1–1.14** after topic 1.14 (3 days)

**40 instruction + 8 assessment/review = 48 total**
