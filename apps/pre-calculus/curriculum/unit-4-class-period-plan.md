# Unit 4 Class-Period Plan

This is the CED-backed class-period plan for AP Precalculus Unit 4. Unit 4 is not assessed on the AP Exam. No local Passwater source exists; pacing is derived from CED topic descriptions and lesson.md files.

## Planning Source Hierarchy

1. CED topic and competency identity from `source/college-board/ced-topic-index.json`.
2. CED clarification/guidance errata from `source/college-board/clarification-guidance.md`.
3. Lesson.md instructional evidence from `modules/unit-04-parameters-vectors-matrices/lesson-4-*/lesson.md`.

### Pacing Rationale

- Topics 4.1–4.4 (Parametric Functions, motion, rates of change, circles/lines): 2 days each = 8 instruction days
- Topic 4.5 (Implicitly Defined Functions): 2 days
- Topic 4.6 (Conic Sections): 3 days (rich topic with four conic types and completing the square)
- Topic 4.7 (Parametrization): 2 days
- Topics 4.8–4.9 (Vectors, vector-valued functions): 3 + 2 = 5 instruction days
- Topics 4.10–4.14 (Matrices): 2 days each = 10 instruction days
- Quiz after parametric block (4.1–4.4): 1 non-instruction day
- Test after parametric/conic block (4.5–4.7): 1 non-instruction day
- Test after matrix block (4.10–4.13): 1 non-instruction day
- Final Unit 4 test (4.1–4.14): 1 non-instruction day
