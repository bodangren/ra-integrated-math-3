# Unit 3 Class-Period Plan

This is the source-backed class-period plan for AP Precalculus Unit 3: Trigonometric and Polar Functions.

## Planning Source Hierarchy

1. CED topic and competency identity from `source/college-board/ced-topic-index.json`.
2. CED clarification/guidance errata from `source/college-board/clarification-guidance.md`.
3. Passwater local instructional evidence from `source/passwater/unit-3.md`.

## Pacing Rationale

Unit 3 has 15 CED topics (3.1–3.15). The Passwater source shows wide variation in content depth: topic 3.1 has 9 worksheets (A–G), topic 3.6 has ~12 notes pages, topic 3.10 has 4 worksheets. One CED topic per day is insufficient. Pacing is driven by Passwater notes page counts, worksheet counts, and the FRQ 3/4 alignment windows.
