# Unit 4 – Lesson Planning Matrix (Payroll in Motion)

> Focus: Payroll calculations, gross-to-net pay, taxes, and compliance. Students apply lookups, logical functions, and formatting to model payroll registers and link to prior statements.

**Teacher Notes**
- Align tax examples with local tables (simplified rates).  
- Ensure students understand both employee and employer sides of payroll entries.  
- Encourage bilingual or clear formatting for stubs.  
- Chart suggestion: bar graph comparing gross, deductions, and net.  
- Connect payroll cash outflow to the ongoing **Cash Summary** from previous units.
