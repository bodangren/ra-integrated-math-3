# Unit 1 Manifest: Balance by Design

## Unit Overview
- **ID:** U01
- **Title:** Balance by Design
- **Description:** Introduction to the accounting equation (A=L+E) and its application in creating a Balance Snapshot for a business.
- **Narrative Context:** Sarah Chen needs to organize her startup's initial financial data to understand what she owns, what she owes, and what she's truly worth.

## Lesson Sequence

| Lesson ID | Type | Title | Description |
|-----------|------|-------|-------------|
| U01L01 | launch | Unit Launch: Why Balance Matters | Intro video, unit problem, familiar solutions, and a low-floor simulation that introduces why balance matters. |
| U01L02 | accounting | Classifying the Business | Accounting Principle 1: classify accounts into Assets, Liabilities, and Equity. |
| U01L03 | accounting | Business Events & The Equation | Accounting Principle 2: apply dual-impact reasoning to business events. |
| U01L04 | accounting | Building the Balance Sheet | Domain Concept 3 (accounting for Unit 1): organize accounts into a readable Balance Sheet structure. |
| U01L05 | excel | Spreadsheet Red Flags | Excel Concept 1: use Conditional Formatting to surface ledger problems. |
| U01L06 | excel | Data Quality & Validation | Excel Concept 2: use Data Validation and workbook controls to prevent ledger drift. |
| U01L07 | excel-project | Whole-Class Guided Build: Balance Snapshot | Whole-class scaffolded workbook build using the shared Unit 1 dataset, foundational how-to, and polish how-to. |
| U01L08 | project | Group Build: Six Dataset Challenge | Six groups each receive a new dataset and build the same Balance Snapshot product with foundational guidance. |
| U01L09 | project | Group Polish: Investor-Ready Snapshot | Groups continue the Lesson 8 workbook and apply the advanced polish/proficiency guide. |
| U01L10 | project | Class Presentation: Balance by Design | Groups present the finished workbook product and explain the business story to the class. |
| U01L11 | assessment | Unit 1 Mastery Check | Three-level summative assessment: knowledge, understanding, application. |

## Canonical Unit 1 Redesign Notes

- Lesson 4 remains accounting-led for Unit 1 because the Balance Sheet structure is the last missing accounting concept before the Excel build sequence begins.
- Lessons 5-6 introduce the Excel control concepts students need before the guided project build.
- Lesson 7 is the common class build: every student uses the same scaffolded workbook and the same dataset.
- Lesson 8 introduces six differentiated group datasets for the same final product structure.
- Lesson 9 is reserved for polish and advanced communication quality, not generic group work.
- Lesson 10 is explicitly a presentation lesson, not only rehearsal/submission time.
