# Accounting + Excel Curriculum Overview (High-Level)

| Unit | Title | Accounting Focus | Excel Focus | Major Deliverable |
|:----:|--------|------------------|--------------|--------------------|
| 1 | **Balance by Design** | Accounting Equation (A=L+E), Account Types | Excel Tables, SUMIF, Conditional Formatting | Mini Balance Sheet proving A=L+E |
| 2 | **Flow of Transactions** | Debit/Credit logic (DEA LER), T-Accounts | IF, **SWITCH**, SUMIFS, Conditional Formats | T-accounts + mini Income Statement (trial balance check only) |
| 3 | **Statements in Balance** | Income + Balance Sheet Integration, formula-based closing entries | Named Ranges, XLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH | Two-statement model + mini Operating Cash Summary |
| 4 | **Payroll in Motion** | Payroll calculations, withholdings, taxes | XLOOKUP, IF, Rounding, Formatting | Payroll register + gross-to-net analysis (update cash summary: payroll cash out) |
| 5 | **Assets That Age** | Depreciation (SLN, DDB), Accumulated Depreciation | SLN, DDB, Linked Schedules | Depreciation schedule + updated financials (update cash summary: add back depreciation) |
| 6 | **Inventory & Project Costing Intelligence** | FIFO/LIFO, Turnover, Cost Flow, Project WIP costing | INDEX/MATCH, Dynamic Arrays, Sparklines | Inventory & Project Cost Dashboard + trendline (update cash summary: working-capital changes) |
| 7 | **Financing the Future** | Loans, Member Equity, Distributions, Financing Options | PMT, IPMT, CHOOSE, Goal Seek | Loan amortization & financing cash flow model (update cash summary: financing inflow/outflow) |
| 8 | **Integrated Model Sprint** | 3-Statement Integration & Scenario Analysis | CHOOSE, Data Tables, Goal Seek, Dashboard Design, Charts | Full dashboard with linked financials |
| Capstone | **Investor-Ready Plan** | Student-created business plan & model | Integration of all skills | Final business plan & investor pitch |

# TechStart / Sarah CEO Narrative Integration

**Protagonist:** Sarah Chen, founder of *TechStart Solutions*, a small digital marketing agency.

| Unit | Sarah’s Business Situation | Narrative Connection | Business Growth Focus |
|:-----|-----------------------------|----------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Just launched TechStart, tracking everything in notebooks. | Realizes chaos of manual tracking; builds first Balance Sheet. | Foundations of financial organization. |
| 2 | Handles first paying clients; confused by revenue timing. | Learns debit/credit flow; sees that profit and cash differ. | Understanding operations and performance. |
| 3 | Applies for a business loan; bank asks for real statements. | Builds professional Balance Sheet + Income Statement. | Financial credibility and structure. |
| 4 | Hires first part-time employee. | Learns payroll compliance and effects on cash flow. | Leadership and payroll management. |
| 5 | Upgrades office equipment; accountant introduces depreciation. | Calculates asset value loss and links to tax savings. | Capital investment management. |
| 6 | Takes on client projects involving materials and subcontractors. | Builds hybrid **inventory + project costing** model for client and self. | Efficiency, pricing, and cost control. |
| 7 | Considers small loan or capital contribution to grow. | Models debt vs. owner contributions and future conversion plan. | Financing growth strategically. |
| 8 | Prepares for Series A pitch. | Builds integrated model showing 12-month projections. | Investor readiness and professional communication. |
| Capstone | Presents business plan and live model to investors. | Synthesizes all learning to show mastery. | Entrepreneurial confidence and decision-making. |

# Excel Curriculum Details

| Unit | New Excel Skills | Visual Formatting Strand | Visualization Strand | Cumulative Skills Reinforced |
|:-----|-------------------|---------------------------|----------------------|-----------------------------|
| 1 | Excel Tables, SUM, SUMIF, structured references | Table design, cell styles, currency formatting | Static bar chart (Assets vs Liabilities) | Basic math, clean formatting |
| 2 | IF, **SWITCH(account_type, \"Asset\", \"Debit\", \"Liability\", \"Credit\", \"Equity\", \"Credit\")**, SUMIFS | Conditional formatting (debit/credit color) | Pie chart (revenue vs expenses) | Logic formulas, T-accounts |
| 3 | Named ranges, XLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, closing logic | Template layout & alignment standards | Stacked column or waterfall chart: operating cash bridge | Cross-sheet linking, data validation |
| 4 | XLOOKUP, nested IF, rounding | Payroll stub layout, highlight taxes | Bar chart: gross → net | Lookup accuracy, formatting professionalism |
| 5 | SLN, DDB, linking schedules | Accumulated depreciation formatting | Line chart: asset value decline | Financial functions integration |
| 6 | INDEX, MATCH, SORT, FILTER, dynamic arrays | Conditional bars for stock levels, project costing visuals | Sparklines: inventory & project trends | Array logic, chart linkage |
| 7 | PMT, IPMT, CHOOSE, Goal Seek | Financial schedule styling | Amortization chart | Financial modeling & what-if tools |
| 8 | CHOOSE, Data Tables, Goal Seek, Dashboard charts, KPI visuals | Color themes, KPI indicators | Combined dashboard with sparklines | Integrated modeling, scenario testing |
| Capstone | Integration of all skills | Professional formatting & layout | Full dashboard storytelling | Comprehensive mastery demonstration |
